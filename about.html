Product Designer · Design Systems

I design products that scale — and systems that hold them together.

I'm a product designer with 5+ years building B2B SaaS tools. Most of that time was spent at Bitsight — a cybersecurity company where design decisions have real consequences for security teams managing hundreds of vendors at once.

I care most about the work that happens between wireframes and shipping: the alignment conversations, the edge-case inventory, the moment a token system makes a color-blind mode trivially easy to add. Good design is often invisible. Broken design always isn't.

Outside of product work, I'm drawn to typography, editorial design, and building things that feel considered — not assembled.
